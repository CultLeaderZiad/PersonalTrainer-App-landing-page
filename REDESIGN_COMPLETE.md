## Design System Refinements

### Colors
- **Background**: Deep charcoal (`oklch(0.08 0 0)`)
- **Accent**: Warm amber (`oklch(0.74 0.189 84.429)`)
- **Text**: Bright white for primary, reduced opacity for secondary
- **Glass Effects**: Semi-transparent overlays with amber borders

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Geist Sans at sizes up to 7xl with proper scaling
- **Body**: Consistent line heights (1.5-1.6) for readability
- **Weights**: Bold for headings, semibold for emphasis

### Glass Morphism
- Applied to all cards and backgrounds
- 20% opacity borders with accent color
- Backdrop blur for depth effect
- Semi-transparent backgrounds that brighten on hover

### Animations
- Fade-in effects on load
- Hover state transitions (300ms standard)
- Scroll indicators with bounce effect
- Shine effect on buttons
- Scale animations on interaction
